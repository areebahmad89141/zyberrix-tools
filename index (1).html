Free PDF tools for everyone

ZyberriX Tools is a free, privacy-first collection of PDF utilities built for people who need fast, reliable file processing without signups or uploading sensitive documents to unknown servers.

Our Mission: Make professional-grade PDF tools accessible to everyone, for free, forever.

Why ZyberriX Tools?

  • 100% Private — Your files never leave your device
  • No Signup Required — Use any tool instantly
  • No Watermarks — Clean, professional output
  • Always Free — Supported by advertising
  • Works Offline — Once loaded, works without internet

Our Tools

  • PDF Compress — Reduce file sizes for faster sharing
  • PDF Merge — Combine multiple PDFs into one
  • PDF Split — Extract pages or split into separate files
  • PDF to JPG — Convert PDF pages to images
  • PDF to Word — Extract text from PDFs
  • Word to PDF — Convert Word/text documents to PDF

Technology

Built using PDF.js, pdf-lib, Mammoth.js, and JSZip — all running entirely client-side for maximum privacy and speed.
